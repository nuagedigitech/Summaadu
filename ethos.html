Our Ethos

TO CREATE EQUALITY IN SOCIETY

  • All India & All Indians should be free of Hunger.
  • All India & All Indians should come under the same Law.
  • All India & All Indians should come under the same Education System which should be at par with global standards & be Free.
  • All India & All Indians should come under a National Health Service which should be at High Standards & be Free.
  • All India & All Indians should be given Employment opportunities according to their skills.
  • There should be Transparency in all Government Departments (Except Defense) like Education, Employment etc.
  • All India & All Indians should come under a Wealth Limitation which should not be more than 10-15 crores.
  • Individual's Salary should be at par with the President's Salary.
